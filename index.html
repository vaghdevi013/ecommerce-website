About Us

Founded in 2016, Attire by Akanksha is the brainchild of the dynamic brother-sister duo, Akanksha and Jayant Sethi. With humble beginnings and zero investment, they embarked on a journey to revolutionize the fashion industry, focusing on creating a diverse range of styles and collections for Indian women. At Attire, the mission is to cater to women seeking apparel that complements their unique body shapes, personalities, and preferences without compromising on aesthetic appeal. The brand believes that every woman deserves to express her individuality through her clothing, and our brand is committed to making that vision a reality.
